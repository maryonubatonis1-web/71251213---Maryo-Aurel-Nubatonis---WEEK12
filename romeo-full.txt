Romeo and Juliet

Act 1 Scene 1

Two households, both alike in dignity,
In fair Verona, where we lay our scene,
From ancient grudge break to new mutiny,
Where civil blood makes civil hands unclean.

Romeo, Romeo, wherefore art thou Romeo?
Deny thy father and refuse thy name.

Romeo doth speak of love and Juliet doth speak of fate.
And the story of Romeo and Juliet begins in Verona.

Act 1 Scene 2

But soft, what light through yonder window breaks?
It is the east, and Juliet is the sun.
Romeo loves Juliet and Juliet loves Romeo.

Thou art thyself, though not a Montague.
Romeo and Juliet speak beneath the moon.

Act 1 Scene 3

The nurse speaks of Juliet and her future.
Juliet thinks of love and destiny and Romeo.

Act 1 Scene 4

Romeo walks with friends in Verona streets.
The name of Romeo echoes in the city of Verona.

Act 1 Scene 5

Juliet meets Romeo at the feast.
Romeo sees Juliet and falls in love.
Juliet sees Romeo and feels the same.
